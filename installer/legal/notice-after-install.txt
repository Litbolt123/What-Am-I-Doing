WHERE FILES GO
--------------------------------
* Program and uninstaller: under your profile, e.g.  %LocalAppData%\Programs\WhatAmIDoing\
* Activity data and logs (after the app runs successfully):  %LocalAppData%\WhatAmIDoing\

Start the app from the Start menu or the desktop shortcut. Look for the tray icon near the clock (on Windows 11 you may need to click ^ to show hidden icons). Left-click the icon to open the dashboard.

If you enabled "Start when I sign in to Windows", the app will start minimized to the tray at your next sign-in.
